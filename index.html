About Me

Hello! I am Miao Yang, a Ph.D. candidate at the School of Materials Science and Engineering, Tianjin University (expected graduation: June 2026). My research focuses on Transient Liquid Phase (TLP) bonding of dissimilar materials, with particular interest in microstructure control, brittle phase elimination, and high-temperature mechanical reliability of bonded joints.

I work on both experimental characterization (SEM, EBSD, TEM, EPMA) and computational simulation (Thermo-Calc, DFT, MD) to understand the process–microstructure–property relationships in advanced structural materials for energy and aerospace applications.
